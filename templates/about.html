How it works

Resume-to-offer in seven steps

  1. Start: Candidates register and log in.
  2. Upload: Resumes are captured as text.
  3. NLP Parsing: We tokenize and normalize key terms.
  4. Match: Overlap scoring compares skills to job descriptions.
  5. Rank: Applications are ordered by match score.
  6. Review: Admins review summaries and status.
  7. Decision & Notify: Mark selected or rejected and notify.

Use cases

Candidate

  • Browse and apply to jobs
  • Upload and reuse resumes
  • Track application status

Admin

  • Post jobs and view applicants
  • Screen with match scores
  • Generate quick reports
